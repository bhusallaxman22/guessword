How to Play GUESSWORD

Find the hidden 4-letter word in 8 tries. Advance through levels by guessing correctly!

After each guess, you'll see two scores:

  • G letters correct & in the right spot.
  • Y letters present but wrong spot.
Controls:
  • Virtual Keyboard: Tap the letters or use "Hide/Show Keyboard" button
  • Keyboard Shortcuts:
    • Ctrl/Cmd + K: Toggle virtual keyboard
    • Enter: Submit guess
    • Escape: Clear input
Examples:

Guess: W O R K Score: 2 1

2 right spot, 1 wrong spot.
